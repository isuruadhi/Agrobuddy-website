Research Objectives

Main Objectives

  1. To provide farmers with innovative and sustainable pest control methods that reduce reliance on chemical pesticides.
  2. To incorporate traditional remedies for crop diseases and pests into the knowledge base of Agro Buddy.
  3. To improve crop yields and reduce the environmental impact of crop management practices.
  4. To enhance the long-term health and sustainability of crops and the agricultural ecosystem.
  5. To create a cost-effective and accessible solution that can be easily used by farmers in rural and remote areas.
  6. To use AI-powered algorithms to provide personalized recommendations to farmers.

Sub Objectives - Isuru Adhikari
