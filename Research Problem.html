Research Problem

  • Lack of focus on traditional remedies:
    While there are many crop management systems and mobile applications available, few incorporate traditional remedies for crop diseases and pests. Research could focus on identifying the effectiveness of traditional remedies and how they can be integrated into modern crop management systems like Agro Buddy.

  • Limited adoption of sustainable pest control methods:
    Despite the potential benefits of sustainable pest control methods, many farmers continue to rely on chemical pesticides. Research could focus on identifying the barriers to adoption of sustainable pest control methods and how Agro Buddy can be designed to address these barriers.

  • Limited AI-powered solutions for smallholder farmers:
    While precision agriculture technologies and AI-powered crop management systems are available, they are often expensive and not accessible to smallholder farmers. Research could focus on how Agro Buddy can be designed to be accessible and affordable to smallholder farmers while still providing personalized recommendations using AI-powered algorithms.

  • Limited user-centered design for crop management systems:
    Many crop management systems are designed without considering the needs and perspectives of the farmers who will be using them. Research could focus on how Agro Buddy can be designed with a user-centered approach, incorporating feedback from farmers and ensuring that the interface is easy to use and understand.

  • Limited research on the effectiveness of mobile applications for crop management:
    While there are many mobile applications available for crop management, there is limited research on their effectiveness. Research could focus on evaluating the impact of Agro Buddy on crop yields, environmental impact, and farmer livelihoods.
