Milestones

31st of March, 2023
Our project proposal presentation has been successfully delivered to the team. During the presentation, we covered all the essential elements crucial in project planning. We began with a strong introduction that emphasized the project's significance, its objectives, and why it's a vital undertaking. The scope and objectives were clearly defined, ensuring everyone had a clear understanding of what we aim to achieve. We provided a detailed breakdown of the project's budget, a well-structured timeline with key milestones, and an overview of the required resources. We also addressed potential risks and presented a robust mitigation strategy. To conclude, we delivered a compelling call to action, urging all stakeholders to support and invest in the project. This group presentation is instrumental in securing the necessary support and resources for the successful execution of our project.

23rd of May, 2023>
Our progress presentation, "Progress Presentation I," has been successfully delivered to the panel. During the presentation, we provided an update on the current status of our project. We highlighted the milestones we have achieved so far, the challenges we've encountered, and how we've addressed them. We also discussed any deviations from the original project plan and how we plan to adjust moving forward. Our team emphasized the importance of staying on track and the measures we've taken to ensure the project's success. This progress presentation serves as a valuable checkpoint for the team to align our efforts and keep the project on the right path.

Our final assessment and viva presentation have been successfully completed. This marked the culmination of our project's journey. During the presentation, we provided a comprehensive overview of the entire project's lifecycle. We highlighted the objectives we set out to achieve and the milestones we successfully reached. This final assessment and viva presentation was a pivotal moment in the project's closure, emphasizing its success and the knowledge gained throughout the process.
